## 🛡️ The Analyst Role (Presenter A)
*The First Line of Defense. Focuses on identifying and signaling threats.*

### 1. Real-Time Signal Monitoring
- **How to explain**: "As an analyst, my primary landing page is the Real-Time Dashboard. I don't just see transactions; I see **Color-Coded Risk Flags** generated by our Python ML service."
- **Action**: Highlight the Transaction List and point out a "High Risk" entry.

### 2. Deep-Dive Fraud Analysis
- **How to explain**: "If I see a suspicious flag, I use the **ML Insights** and **Location Analytics**. For example, if a user logs in from New York while their card is active in London, the system flags a 'Proximity Alert' for me."
- **Action**: Navigate to the ML Insights or Location Analytics components.

### 3. Escalation (The "Action" Step)
- **How to explain**: "When I confirm a threat, I use the **Central Support Terminal**. I don't just send an email—I send an encrypted transmission directly to Administration to request a 'Soft Freeze' on the account."

## ⚙️ The Admin Role (Presenter B)
*The System Overseer. Focuses on governance, infrastructure, and responding to signals.*

### 1. Personnel Provisioning (Identity Management)
- **How to explain**: "Admins manage the 'Identity & Access' plane. We provision new analysts, define their clearance levels, and ensure only authorized personnel can view sensitive financial telemetry."
- **Action**: Show the `User Management` table and the "Propose New Analyst" modal.

### 2. Reviewing Clearance Requests
- **How to explain**: "I monitor the **Access Requests** tab. When an analyst sends a signal (like the one Presenter A just demonstrated), I receive a notification badge on my dashboard."
- **Action**: Check the "Access Requests" tab and show the red notification badge.

### 3. Protocol Execution (Responding)
- **How to explain**: "I can 'Review Issue' to see the analyst's justification. I then provide an **Administrative Response** which the analyst will see on their end, closing the loop on the investigation."
- **Action**: Open a request, type a response, and click "Resolve & Send".

### 4. System Governance & Purging
- **How to explain**: "Finally, I ensure the audit trail is clean. I can purge old transmission logs or de-provision accounts if a contract ends, maintaining total system hygiene."
- **Action**: Demonstrate the "Purge Record" (Trash icon) or "De-provision" (Delete user) steps.
